About Us

Glamourous Nails is a premier nail salon that lets you avail the best pampering service. We provide the ultimate treatment so that you always feel happy. Our rejuvenating spa cum Nail Salon takes pride in having a distinct ambiance where you can pamper yourself after a hectic day's end.
We uphold the Highest Standards of Nail Beauty, take care of Hygiene and Health and ensure safety of the clients. On top of that, we deliver the best Nail Beauty through our highly trained Professionals.
At Glamourous Nails, everyone feels at home, with relaxing treatments. Our treatments range from head to toe. We are specialized in nails, nail art and offer the latest designs and products available in the industry. Apart from this we provide other pampering service on the rest of the body including manicures, pedicures, waxing, eyelash extensions and more.
